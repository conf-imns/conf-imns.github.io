Call for Posters and Demos

The IMNS 2026 invites submissions of technical posters and demos on topics including but not limited to multimedia systems, networking, communications, security, AI/machine learning, IoT, and smart applications. The goal is to provide an interactive forum for presenting emerging work, novel ideas, and practical systems, fostering collaboration between academia and industry.

Scope and Participation

The posters and demos session provides an excellent opportunity for researchers to engage directly with attendees, showcase prototypes, and receive valuable feedback. We encourage submissions of work-in-progress, early-stage research, and industry prototypes.

Accepted posters and demos will be published in the IMNS 2026 conference proceedings. At least one author of each accepted poster or demo is required to register at full rate and present in person.

Submission Instructions

Submit a two-page abstract following the IEEE conference template through EDAS.

  • Present the problem, significance, main idea, and results.
  • For demos: explain what will be shown and interaction details.
  • Include all authors' names, affiliations, and email addresses.
  • Indicate Poster: or Demo: in the title.
  • Max 2 pages (3rd page allowed only for demo special arrangements).
